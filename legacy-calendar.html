POST 07 · SAT 18 C
Education Carousel

“Your cat can’t sweat like you can.”

Pain point: Gulf summer heatstroke & dehydration. Cats cool almost entirely through panting and paw-pad sweat; tile/balcony surfaces can run 40–60°F hotter than the air.

Hirra tie-in: Free offline emergency card carries the heat checklist; daily mood/appetite check-ins catch the lethargy that precedes an episode.

Read full caption

Cats are not built for this heat. They cool by panting and a little paw-pad sweat, nothing like us. Save this before the next 45-degree day, and check the ground before she steps onto it this afternoon. Not a diagnosis, a reason to act in the first five minutes.

القطط لم تُخلق لهذا الحر. تُبرّد نفسها باللهاث وقليل من العرق عبر وسائد قدميها، لا شيء يشبه ما نملكه نحن. احفظي هذا قبل يوم الخامسة والأربعين القادم، وتفقّدي الأرض قبل أن تطأها بعد ظهر اليوم.

POST 08 · TUE 21 F
Cat & culture Single photo

“Her flat little face.”

Pain point: Persian/Himalayan flat-faced anatomy makes cooling and breathing harder. Persian is the single most popular breed in Saudi Arabia.

Hirra tie-in: Breed-aware baseline weighs panting or open-mouth breathing as a stronger signal for flat-faced cats.

Read full caption

If she is a Persian or Himalayan, her beautiful flat face makes her work harder to cool down and to breathe than a shorthair does, especially this time of year. A little snoring while she naps is usually just her. Open-mouth breathing or real effort is not. Save this for the next hot afternoon.

إن كانت قطتك من فصيلة الشيرازي أو الهيمالايا، فوجهها المسطّح الجميل يجعلها تبذل جهداً أكبر للتبرّد والتنفّس من قطة قصيرة الشعر. الشخير الخفيف أثناء قيلولتها غالباً طبيعي لها. أما التنفّس بفم مفتوح أو بجهد واضح فمختلف.

Education Carousel

“If your boy is in and out of the litter box today…”

Pain point: urinary blockage in male cats is a true 24–48 hour emergency; the highest-risk profile matches most Gulf apartment cats.

Hirra tie-in: exactly the pattern the daily litter check-in and the WarningEngine’s straining flag are built to catch, days before a crisis.

Read full caption

This one is for owners of male cats especially. Straining, tiny amounts, more trips to the box than usual, together those three are not a symptom to sit with. Save this, and if you see it today, call your vet today.

هذا المنشور لأصحاب القطط الذكور خصوصاً. الإجهاد عند التبوّل، كميات ضئيلة جداً، وزيارات لصندوق الرمل أكثر من المعتاد؛ هذه الثلاثة معاً ليست عرَضاً يُنتظر.
